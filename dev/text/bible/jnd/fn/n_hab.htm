THE HOLY BIBLE Darby's Translation
Darby's Notes on Habakkuk
1:1a *
Said to mean. 'Embracing.'
1:4b justice
Or 'judgment.'
1:5c work
A thing done, as Isa. 5:12.
1:8d prance
Or 'spread themselves.'
1:9e crowd
Or 'the striving,' 'eagerness.'
1:9f forwards
Others 'eastwards.'
1:11g mind
Lit. 'spirit,' or 'state of soul.'
1:11h guilty
Or 'Then will he sweep by as the wind, and pass on and make himself guilty.'
1:16a dainty
Or 'plenteous.'
2:3b delay
Or 'be behindhand.'
2:5c man
Geber. Job 3:3.
2:5d desire
Or 'appetite;' lit. 'soul:' see Isa. 5:14.
2:6e pledges
There is a play on the word, which may also mean 'thick clay.' So in ver. 7 'bite' may mean 'exact usury of:' cf. Jer. 8:17; Ezek. 22:12.
2:10f against
Or 'endangered.'
2:13g nations
Leummim, Ps. 2:1.
2:13h vain
Or 'to no purpose:' see Jer. 51:58; Isa. 30:7.
2:15i flask
Or 'thy poison,' 'thy fury.'
2:16k of
Or 'more than with.'
2:16l foreskin
Or 'thine uncircumcision.'
2:17m afraid
Or 'shall affright thee.'
2:17a land
Or 'earth.'
3:1b Shigionoth
Plural of Shiggaion: see title of Ps. 7.
3:2c work
Poal: as ch. 1:5.
3:3d full
Or 'was full.'
3:4e Rays
Lit. 'horns.'
3:6f earth
Or 'made the earth to tremble.'
3:6g everlasting
Or 'His are the ways of old.'
3:14h leaders
Others read 'the leaders of his hordes.'
3:16a invader
Or 'his overcomer.'
3:16b people
Or 'when he that rusheth in troops upon us shall come up against the people.' Cf. Gen. 49:19.
3:19c Lord
Jehovah Adonai: see Ezek. 2:4.
