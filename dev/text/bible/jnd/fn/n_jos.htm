THE HOLY BIBLE Darby's Translation
Darby's Notes on Joshua
1:1a Joshua
Meaning 'Jehovah is Saviour:' see Num. 13:16.
1:11b victuals
As 'venison,' Gen. 27:3.
1:14a array
See Ex. 13:18.
1:18b commandment
Lit. 'mouth.'
1:18c us
Or 'him.'
2:1d harlot's
Lit. 'the house of a woman, a harlot.'
2:9e faint
'Melt away,' so ver. 24.
2:10f destroyed
Same word as 'devoted to destruction.'
2:12g sign
Lit. 'sign of truth:' see ver. 14.
3:10a the
Or 'a.'
3:16b plain
The Arabah: see chs. 11:2, 16; 12:1, 3; 18:18; Deut. 1:1. Meaning strictly 'dry-land,' but applied to the narrow strip of land in the Jordan valley on both sides of the river below the lake of Gennesaret. In ch. 4:13 it seems to mean the parts of the Arabah about Jericho.
4:6c hereafter
Lit. 'to-morrow;' and so ver. 21.
4:18a previously
See Gen. 31:2.
4:24b continually
Lit. 'all the days.'
5:2a, 5:3a stone-knives
Lit. 'swords of rocks.' Some read 'sharp knives.'
5:3b Araloth
Or 'of the foreskins'.
5:9c Gilgal
'Rolling.'
5:11d corn
Or 'store-corn,' i.e. from the harvest of the year before.
5:13e for
Or 'with.'
5:14f come
Or 'for I, the captain (or prince) of the army of Jehovah, am now come.'
6:4a, 6:6a, 6:8a, 6:13a blast-trumpets, 6:5a blast-horn
See Note, Lev. 25:10.
6:17b accursed
'Devoted by curse to destruction:' so ver. 18.
6:26a In, in
Or 'on.' *Meaning, 'in the death of:' see 1Kings 16:34.
7:15a wickedness
Properly 'ungodliness.'
7:19b confession
Or 'give praise.'
7:24c, 7:26c Achor
Meaning, 'trouble.'
8:14b place
Or 'time.'
8:14c plain
The Arabah: see ch. 3:16.
8:20a power
Lit 'hand:' see Deut. 32:36.
8:26b destroyed
See ch. 2:10.
8:33c officers
See Deut. 1:15.
8:33d beginning
Or 'had in the beginning commanded.'
8:35a lived
Lit. 'walked.'
9:1b lowland
The Shephelah: see Deut. 1:7.
9:2c accord
Lit. 'mouth.'
9:4d journey
Or 'feigned themselves ambassadors.' Some read 'provided themselves with victuals.'
9:6e make
Lit. 'cut:' so regularly, as Gen. 15:18.
9:7f I
Or 'we,' i.e. Israel.
9:12g mouldy
Others read 'in crumbs.'
9:23a be
Lit. 'there shall not be cut off from you.'
9:24b lives
Lit. 'our souls.'
10:5c armies
Elsewhere also 'camps.'
10:12a still
Lit. 'be silent.'
10:13b Jasher
Or 'of the upright,' as 2Sam. 1:18.
10:40a mountain
Or 'hill-country;' and so chs. 11:16, 21; 12:8; 18:12; 21:11.
10:40b south
It appears that the border land between the country that was afterwards allotted to Judah, and the wilderness, was called Negeb, which means 'the South.' See Gen. 12:9.
11:2a Chinneroth
Gennesaret.
11:2b west
Lit 'the sea.'
11:4c armies
Or 'camps.'
11:6d slain
Lit. 'pierced through.'
11:8e valley
Wide valley or plain surrounded by heights: so ver. 17.
11:13f hills
Or 'mounds:' see Jer. 30:18.
11:14g men
Lit. 'The Adam' -- man as a race.
11:15h nothing
Or 'not a word.'
11:16i south
The Negeb: see ch. 10:40
11:16k lowland
The Shephelah: see ch. 9:1; Deut. 1:7.
11:16l plain
The Arabah: so ch. 12:8, &c.: see ch. 3:16.
11:17a, 12:7a mountain
Or 'Mount Halak.'
11:21b mountains, mountain
Or 'hill-country.'
12:1c, 12:3a plain
The Arabah: so ch. 12:8, &c.: see ch. 3:16.
12:2d river, ravine
So ch. 13:9, 16: see Note, Deut. 2:36.
12:3e and
Or 'and over.'
12:3f Chinneroth
See ch. 11:2.
12:3g Pisgah
Or 'the hill-slopes;' and so ch. 13:20, &c.; Deut. 3:17.
12:4h giants
'Rephaim:' so chs. 13:12; 17:15; Deut. 2:11.
12:18a Lasharon
According to some, 'of the Sharon:' see Acts 9:35, &c.
12:23b Goim
Or 'the nations,' as Gen. 14:1.
13:1c age
Lit. 'days.'
13:3d lordships
Heb. Sehren, a word used only for the 'lords of the Philistines,' here and elsewhere.
13:3e Ashdod
Called 'Azotus' in Acts 8:40.
13:8f him
i.e. the other half of Manasseh.
13:17a Bamoth-Baal
High places of Baal.
13:23b, 13:28b hamlets
Enclosures, farm-houses: so Ex. 8:13 and elsewhere.
13:27c Chinnereth
See ch. 11:2.
13:30d villages
See Num. 32:41.
13:32e Jericho
Or, as Num. 22:1, 'on the other side of Jordan from Jericho.'
14:15a Kirjath-Arba
The city of Arba.
14:15b Anakim
See ch. 11:22; Deut. 2:10; 9:2.
15:4c ended
Lit. 'its out-goings were:' so elsewhere.
15:8a valley
Or 'gorge.'
15:10b Har-jearim
Or 'the mountain of Jearim.'
15:13c Arba
i.e. Kirjath-Arba.
15:19d land
Or 'the south land.'
15:25e Hazor-hadattah
Or 'new Hazor.'
15:25a Hazor
Others, 'and Kirioth (Hezron is Hazor).'
15:32b, 15:45b hamlets
See ch. 13:23.
15:40c Lahmas
Or 'Lahmam'
15:45d villages
Lit. 'daughters:' see ch. 17:11.
15:46e westward
Or 'toward the sea:' so chs. 16:6; 17:9, 10; 19:11, &c.
15:62f Ir-Hammelah
i.e. City of salt.
17:11a villages
Lit. 'daughters:' so throughout.
17:14a great
Or 'numerous;' and so vers. 15, 17.
17:15b down
Or 'clear it.'
17:15c giants
Or 'Rephaim:' see ch. 18:16; Deut. 2:11.
17:18d outgoings
Or 'even to its extremities.'
17:18e for
Or 'though.'
18:5f south
Negeb: see ch. 10:40.
18:18a Arabah
Or 'the plain,' as ch. 3:16; Deut. 1:1.
18:24b Chephar-haammonai
i.e. 'Village of the Ammonites.'
18:28c Jebusi
Lit. 'the Jebusite.'
19:8a Ramah
Meaning 'height.'
19:8b south
Negeb: see ch. 10:40. It may perhaps here be taken as south Ramah, or south Ramoth, as 1Sam. 30:27.
19:13c Neah
Or 'which bounds Neah;' others read 'Rimmon, Methoar, Neah.'
19:27d left
i.e. on the north.
19:33a Zaanannim
Or 'Allon-Zaanannim.'
19:46b Japho
i.e. Joppa.
19:47c with
Or 'went out beyond:' see Judg. 18:1.
19:50d word
Lit. 'mouth:' so chs. 21:3; 22:9.
20:2e Appoint
Lit. 'give.'
20:2f through
Lit. 'by the hand of:' so chs. 21:2, 8; 22:9.
20:3g unwittingly
See Num. 35:11.
20:4a take
Lit. 'gather,' as Deut. 33:5.
20:8b from
See Num. 22:1, &c.
21:11a mountain
Or 'amongst the hills of:' see however ver. 21.
21:45a things
Or 'all the good works.'
22:8b copper
Or 'bronze.'
22:10c districts
Or 'borders.'
22:11a from
Or 'on the other side of.'
22:16b wickedness
Or 'trespass;' as vers. 20, 22, 31; 'unfaithfulness,' Lev. 5:15; Num. 31:16.
22:17c although
Or 'when.'
22:24d future
Lit. 'to-morrow;' and so vers. 27, 28.
22:33a said
Or 'intended,' as Ex. 2:14.
22:34b witness
Heb. Ed.
23:1c age
Lit. 'in days;' and so ver. 2.
23:13a, 23:15a land
Lit. 'ground.'
23:15b fulfilled
Lit. 'hath come upon.'
24:2c river
Euphrates.
24:7a Egypt
Or 'to the Egyptians.'
24:11b men
Strictly, 'masters,' 'possessors,' elsewhere rendered 'citizens:' see Gen. 14:13.
24:14c perfectness
'Sincerely:' see Judg. 9:16, 19.
24:17d bondage
Lit. 'bondmen.'
24:27a words
Strictly, 'sayings,' imrah: see Note, Ps. 119:11.
24:32b portion
Or 'allotment.'
24:32c kesitahs
See Gen. 33:19.
