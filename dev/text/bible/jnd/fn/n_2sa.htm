THE HOLY BIBLE Darby's Translation
Darby's Notes on 2 Samuel
1:9a anguish
Others, 'bewilderment.'
1:18b the
Or 'the use of the.'
1:18c Jasher
See Josh. 10:13.
1:21a of
Or 'in.'
1:21b heave-offerings
i.e. which produce the wherewithal for heave-offerings.
1:24c splendour
Or 'delights.'
2:8d Ishbosheth
Esh-Baal 1Chron. 8:33.
2:16a Helkath-hazzurim
Meaning, 'field of sharp swords.'
2:23b belly
Or 'under the fifth rib:' so chs. 3:27; 4:6; 20:10.
2:29c plain
The Arabah; so ch. 4:7: see Josh. 3:16.
3:3a Absalom
Abishalom, 1Kings 15:2.
3:33a fool
Nabal, 'impious,' 'ungodly:' see 1Sam. 25:3.
3:39a though
Lit. 'and.'
3:39b me
Or 'are more powerful than I.'
4:3c sojourners
Or 'strangers.'
4:4d Mephibosheth
Called also Merib-Baal: 1Chron. 8:34.
4:7e plain
The Arabah: see Josh. 3:16.
5:6a back
Or 'hither, unless thou drivest away the blind and lame.'
5:8b and
Others read, 'let him cast into the watercourse both the lame and.'
5:8c ...
For the complement of the passage see 1Chron. 11:6.
5:9d Millo
The rampart or citadel.
5:20e Baal-perazim
Meaning, 'place of breaches.'
5:24a army
Or 'camp.'
6:2b from
Or 'to.'
6:2c between
Or 'upon:' see 1Sam. 4:4.
6:5d wood
Others, 'with all their might and with singing:' see 1Chron. 13:8.
6:6e stumbled
or 'broken loose,' others, 'slipped.'
6:8f Perez-Uzzah
Meaning, 'breach of Uzzah.'
6:19a wine
Or 'a ration of flesh.'
6:21b appoint
Lit. 'command.'
6:21c ruler
Or 'prince,' as chs. 5:2 and 7:8.
7:16a firm
Or 'made sure.'
7:18b sat
In the sense of 'tarried:' same word as 'abide,' Gen. 24:55; 29:19.
7:19c manner
Lit. 'law.'
7:19d man
Or 'and that after the manner (law) of man.'
7:23e went
'Went' is plural here.
7:23f them
Lit. 'for you.'
7:27g to
Lit. 'uncovered the ear of,' as Ruth 4:4; 1Sam. 9:15; 20:2, &c.
7:28h God
Or 'Thou art the Same, -- God:' see Neh. 9:7, and compare Deut. 32:39.
7:28i true
Lit. 'truth.'
8:1k capital
'Power of the capital' is 'Metheg-ha-ammah,' meaning, 'the Bridle of the Mother (city),' i.e. the metropolis: see 2Sam. 20:19; Num. 21:25.
8:3a dominion
Or 're-establish his (David's) power.'
8:10b congratulate
Lit. 'bless.'
8:13c Syrians
Some read 'Edomites.'
8:15d judgment
Or 'right,' mishpat.
8:18e Pelethites
The executioners (a sort of bodyguard), and the runners.
9:12a Mica
See Note e, Judg. 17:4.
10:16a river
i.e. the Euphrates.
11:4b purified
Or 'and she purified.'
11:7c prospered
Lit. 'about the prosperity of ...' (three times repeated).
11:25a encourage
Lit. 'strengthen.'
12:5a death
Lit. 'is a son of death.'
12:15b sick
Or 'sick unto death.'
12:22a thought
Lit. 'said.'
12:24b Solomon
Meaning, 'peaceful.'
12:25c Jedidiah
Meaning, 'beloved of Jehovah.'
13:12a infamy
As 'folly,' 1Sam. 25:25.
13:18b colours
See Gen. 37:3.
13:25c be
Lit. 'and we will not be.'
13:28a, 13:29a servants
Lit. 'young men.'
13:37b day
Or 'all the time.'
14:4c spoke
Or 'came.'
14:6d part
Lit. 'there was no deliverer between.'
14:17a be
Or '... God be.'
14:20b thing
Or 'to give this turn to my speech.'
14:30a mine
Lit. 'at my hand.'
15:1b after
Or 'soon after.'
15:4c to
Or 'round by,' as Ex. 18:13, &c.
15:7d forty
Possibly 'four.'
15:10a emissaries
Lit. 'spies.'
15:12b offered
Lit. 'sacrificed the sacrifices,' see Deut. 12:15.
15:17c house
Or 'at Beth-Merchak,' as 'very far,' Jer. 8:19.
15:19d dwellest
Lit. 'into thy place.'
15:20e thee
Or 'brethren with thee in mercy and faithfulness.'
15:27a seer
Or, as interrogative, 'Dost thou see?'
16:1b servant
Strictly, 'young man.'
17:25a Israelite
Perhaps 'Ishmaelite:' see 1Chron. 2:17.
18:3b for
Some read 'for now thou art worth.'
18:12a you
Others, including LXX, read 'Take care for my sake.'
18:13b concealed
Or 'if I had acted falsely against his life nothing is concealed.'
18:14c spears
Lit. 'rods (staves).'
18:14d body
Lit. 'into the midst (heart) of Absalom.'
18:16e back
Some translate, 'spared.'
18:21f Cushite
Or 'Ethiopian.'
18:23g plain
Lit. 'circle:' see Gen. 13:10.
19:24a washed, trimmed
Lit. 'made.'
19:25b came
Or 'as soon as he came to Jerusalem.'
19:32a great
Or 'rich.'
19:36b way
Or 'time.'
19:37c Chimham
* Meaning 'Longing.'
20:14a together
Some read 'all the young men gathered together.'
20:19b mother
* Or 'a city, even a mother (city) in Israel.' See 2Sam. 8:1; Num. 21:25, 32; 32:42; Josh. 17:11 and Notes.
20:23a Cherethites
The executioners.
20:23b Pelethites
The couriers.
20:24c levy
Elsewhere 'forced labour' or 'tribute.'
20:25d scribe
Or 'secretary.'
21:1e inquired
Lit. 'sought the face.'
21:8f of
i.e. Merab: see 1Sam. 18:19.
21:12a men
Heb. baali, or 'citizens,' as Judg. 9:2, 47.
21:12b place
Or 'broadway.'
21:16c Raphah
Or 'the giant:' see Deut. 2:11.
21:16d shekels
Lit. 'weights.'
21:19e Goliath
Or 'Lahmi, brother of Goliath:' see 1Chron. 20:5.
22:2f rock
High rock.
22:3g rock
Lit. 'God of my rock (or strength).'
22:3h trust
'Trust,' as fleeing for refuge: so ver. 31.
22:6a Sheol
See Note, Gen. 37:35.
22:15b enemies
Lit. 'and scattered them.'
22:24c, 24:26c upright
Or 'perfect,' as Gen. 6:9; 17:1; Deut. 18:13.
22:26d gracious
Chasid, 'pious,' 'godly:' see 2Chron. 6:41. A form of this word is used specifically of the ways of Hezekiah and of Josiah in 2Chron. 32:32 and 35:26. See also Ps. 4:3; 12:1; 16:10.
22:28a dost
Or 'wilt.'
22:31b word
Here imrah. as Deut. 33:9: see Note, Ps. 119:11.
22:33c smooth
Some read, as in Ps. 18:32, 'maketh my way perfect.'
22:45d cringing
See Note to Deut. 33:29.
22:46e close
Or 'fortified.'
22:46f places
Or 'tremble in their strong castles.'
22:51a to
Some read 'He is a tower of deliverance for.'
23:1b saith
See Gen. 22:16.
23:3c among
Or 'over.'
23:3d just
Or 'a just ruler over mankind ' 'the Adam:' see Gen. 1:27, 6:1.
23:5e desire
Or 'all good pleasure.'
23:5f grow
Or 'For all my salvation and all good pleasure, would he not make them to grow?'
23:7g himself
Lit. 'fills his hand.'
23:8h Joseb-Bassebeth
Or 'he who sits in the (first) place.'
23:8i Tachkemonite
Or 'the Hachmonite.'
23:8k against
Or, as 1Chron. 11:11, 'he brandished his spear against.'
23:9l Ahohite
Or 'son of Ahohi.'
23:20a lions
Heb. Ariel, strictly, 'lion of God (El),' i.e. 'great heroes;' and so 1Chron. 11:22.
23:35b Hezrai
See 1Chron. 11:37.
24:5a ravine
or 'valley:' see Deut. 2:13, 36.
24:6b Tahtim-hodshi
Or 'the low country of Hodshi.'
