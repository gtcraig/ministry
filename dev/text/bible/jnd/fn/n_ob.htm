THE HOLY BIBLE Darby's Translation
Darby's Notes on Obadiah
1:1e *
(The title of this book, 'Obadiah'), Said to mean, 'Servant of Jah.'
1:1f vision
Chazan: see Dan. 8:15.
1:3g pride
Or 'presumption.'
1:7a thee
Lit. 'the men of thy peace.'
1:11b substance
Or 'forces.'
1:12c disaster
Or 'strange fate:' see Job 31:3.
1:17d deliverance
Lit. 'escape,' or 'the escaped ones.'
1:19e south
Negeb; and so ver. 20: see Josh. 10:40.
1:19f lowland
Shephelah; see Deut. 1:7.
1:20g captives
Lit. 'captivity.'
