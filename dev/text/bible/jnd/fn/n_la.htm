THE HOLY BIBLE Darby's Translation
Darby's Notes on Lamentations
1:1a *
In chs. 1 and 2 the initial letter of each verse, consisting of three parts or lines, follows the alphabetical order.
1:5b adversaries, adversary
Or 'oppressor,' Tzar; so vers. 7, 10, 17: see Note o, Ps. 8:2, and Ps. 78:61.
1:5c head
See Deut. 28:44.
1:7d wanderings
Or 'miseries:' see ch. 3:19; Isa. 58:7.
1:7e into
Or 'by.'
1:7f ruin
Or 'cessation' or 'days of rest,' and see Lev. 26:43.
1:8g grievously
Lit. 'sinned a sin.'
1:11a, 1:19a soul
Or 'life.'
1:13b prevaileth
Or 'he causeth it to prevail.'
1:15c assembly
Or 'a solemn assembly.'
1:17d him
Or 'Jehovah hath given his adversaries round about charge against Jacob.'
1:18e commandment
Lit. 'his mouth.'
2:3a his
Or 'turned back his' (i.e. Israel's).
2:6b assembly, 2:6b, 2:7b, feast
Or 'solemn assembly.' The same word appears in the original for 'assembly,' ch. 1:15 and here, and set feast. Num. 10:10.
2:8c destroying
Lit. 'swallowing up,' as vers. 2, 5, 16.
2:14a expulsion
Or 'seduction,' and see Jer. 27:10.
2:22b side
See Jer. 6:25.
3:1c *
In this chapter, each verse in every succeeding set of three begins with the same Hebrew letter; and the whole in alphabetical order.
3:1d man
Geber; and so in vers. 27, 35, 39: see Job 3:3.
3:13a arrows
Lit. 'sons.'
3:19b wandering
Or 'my misery:' see ch. 1:7.
3:39c man
Geber, Job 3:3: 'living man' in this verse is Adam.
3:39d punishment
chet; sin, also punishment, Isa. 1:18, 'sins:' see Zech. 14:19.
3:47e, 3:48e ruin
Lit. 'breach:' see Jer. 6:14; 8:21; Amos 6:6.
3:52a me
Or 'Mine enemies have without cause chased me.'
3:53b pit
See Note, Jer. 37:16.
3:58c cause
Lit. 'causes.'
4:1d *
In this chapter the initial Hebrew letter of each verse, consisting of two parts or lines, follows the alphabetical order.
4:6e punishment
* The Hebrew word is used for iniquity, also the punishment of iniquity, as here and ver. 22, and three times in Ezek. 14:10. First used in Gen. 4:13: see Zech. 14:19, where, however, a different word is used. See Lam. 3:39.
4:7f Nazarites
Or 'princes.'
4:15a Unclean
See Isa. 52:11.
4:16b divided
Or 'hath dispersed,' see Gen. 49:7.
5:6c Asshur
i.e. the Assyrians.
5:8d deliverer
Lit. 'none that rendeth away:' see Ps. 136:24.
5:10e gloweth
* Or 'fiery red,' as 'burned,' Gen. 43:30; 'yearned,' 1Kings 3:26; 'kindled,' Hos. 11:8.
5:12f elders
Or 'the aged,' as ch. 4:16.
5:14a music
* Neginoth, 'stringed instruments,' as Title, Ps. 4, 6, 54, 55, 61 ,67, 76. The verb is, 'to strike the strings:' Isa. 38:20; Lam. 3:14; Hab. 3:19.
5:19b dwellest
Or 'sittest:' see Ps. 80:1.
