THE HOLY BIBLE Darby's Translation
Darby's Notes on Job
1:1a Uz
See Lam. 4:21.
1:1b perfect
Complete, lacking nothing; cf. 2Sam. 22:24.
1:3c, 1:16c sheep
'Small cattle,' applying equally to both sheep and goats'
1:5d cursed
So ver. 11, and ch. 2:5, 9; see 1Kings 21:10, 13.
1:6e a
Lit. 'the;' so ver. 13, &c.: see 2Kings 4:18.
1:6f Satan
Meaning 'Adversary.' Lit. 'the Satan,' and so to ch. 2:7.
1:22a God
Or 'nor uttered anything unseemly against God.'
2:4b life
Lit. 'soul.'
3:3a, 3:23a man
Geber: so ch. 4:17; 14:10 (first), 14; 16:21 (first); 22:2 (first); 33:17 (second), 29; 34:7, 9, 34; 38:3; 40:7. It refers to strength, a cognate form (Gibbor) being used for 'mighty men,' 'heroes,' as Gen. 6:4; 2Sam. 23:8; Job 16:14.
3:4b +God
Heb. Eloah: see Note, Gen. 1:1. The word occurs many times in Job.
3:8c Leviathan
See Job 41:1; Ps. 74:14; 104:26; Isa. 27:1.
3:14d themselves
Or 'who ruined buildings.'
3:17e wearied
i.e. lit. 'whose strength is worn out.'

Or 'thy fear of God.'
