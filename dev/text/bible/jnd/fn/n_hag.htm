THE HOLY BIBLE Darby's Translation
Darby's Notes on Haggai
1:1f *
(The title of this Book, 'Haggai'), Said to mean 'Festive' or 'Festival of Jehovah.'
1:1g Darius
Darius Hystaspes: see Zech. 1:1; Ezra 4:24.
1:1h by
Lit. 'by the hand of,' as often.
1:4i wainscoted
Or 'ceiled:' see Jer. 22:14.
1:5k, 1:7k Consider
Lit. 'Set your heart on:' so ch. 2:15, 18, 'set your heart.'
1:10a you
Or 'for your sake.'
1:11b man
Adam -- mankind.
2:14a offer
Lit. 'they present,' as Lev. 1:2; 7:12, &c.
2:15b, 2:18b consider
Lit. 'set your heart.'
2:15c onward
Lit. 'upward;' and so ver. 18. Some take it as meaning 'backward' in ver. 15.
