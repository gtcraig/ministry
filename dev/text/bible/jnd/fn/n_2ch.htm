THE HOLY BIBLE Darby's Translation
Darby's Notes on 2 Chronicles
1:5a tabernacle
Mishkan: see Ex. 25:9.
1:11b, 1:12b honour
Or 'glory.'
1:15c lowland
The Shephelah: see 1Chron. 27:28.
2:1a build
Lit. 'thought to build.'
2:4b dedicate
Lit. 'sanctify.'
2:4c arrangement
As Lev. 24:6.
2:6d sacrifice
Or 'incense.' Lit. 'to burn as incense' i.e. all that is burnt on the altar.
2:10e measures
Heb. Cors: see Note, ch. 27:5.
2:10f wheat
Perhaps 'wheat for food.'
2:11g answered
Lit. 'said.'
2:13h Abi
Or 'of Huram my father;' and so ch. 4:16.
2:14i Dan
* In 1Kings 7:14 Hiram is said to be of the tribe of Naphtali, but here of Dan, probably due to the fact that Naphtali belonged to the camp of Dan: Num. 2:25-31.
3:1a Moriah
Meaning, 'shewn by Jehovah.'
3:1b appeared
Or 'which was shewn.'
3:5c palm-trees
Or 'palms;' and so elsewhere.
3:8d, 3:10d place
Lit. 'holy of holies;' Kodesh: see Note Ex. 25:8.
3:16a oracle
Perhaps we should read 'chains at the collar of the columns.'
3:17b Jachin
'He will establish.'
3:17c Boaz
'In Him is strength.'
4:5d baths
See 1Kings 7:26.
4:9e court
Or 'enclosure,' as ch. 6:13.
4:11f bowls
See Note to 1Kings 7:40.
4:16g Abiv
Or 'Huram his father.'
4:17h plain
'The circle:' see Gen. 13:10.
4:18i vessels
Elsewhere 'utensils.'
4:22a knives
Or 'snuffers.'
4:22b censers
Or 'fire-pans.'
4:22c, 5:7c place
As ch 3:8.
5:5d the
Or 'and the.'
5:9e long
Others, 'And they drew out the staves.'
5:9f are
Lit. 'it is.'
6:2a place
As vers. 30, 33, 39; Ex. 15:17; 1Kings 8:13.
6:13b court
Or 'enclosure,' as ch. 4:9.
6:33a name
See 1Kings 8:43.
6:36a to
Lit. 'before.'
6:36b have
Lit. 'those that take them captive have....'
6:41c saints
Chasid: see Note to ver. 42.
6:41d goodness
Or 'in goodness' or 'in that which is good.'
6:42e mercies
Chesed, whence the word Chasid, 'saint' (ver. 41). It is 'goodness' in God, 'piety' in man towards God, or one's parents, 'loving-kindness,' 'mercy.' Christ Himself, as the One in whom these qualities are found, is called Chasid, 'Holy One:' see 2Sam. 22:26; Ps. 89:1-3, 19.
8:8a tribute-service
Or 'the levy.'
8:14b required
Lit. 'for the thing of the day in its day,' as 1Chron. 16:37, and cf. 2Chron. 31:16.
9:5a affairs
Lit. 'of thy things.'
9:7b men
Enosh: see Gen. 4:26.
9:12c besides
i.e. 'besides that which he had given her in return for.'
9:21a ivory
Or 'elephants' teeth.'
9:24b year
Lit. 'each year the business of the year.'
9:27c lowland
The Shephelah: see Deut. 1:7.
10:11a scorpions
See 1Kings 12:11.
10:19b against
Lit. 'fell away from.'
11:11c captains
Elsewhere, 'prince,' 'ruler.'
11:15a he-goats
or 'demons,' 'satyrs:' see Lev. 17:7.
12:2b transgressed
Strictly, 'acted treacherously:' see Josh. 22:16.
12:7c deliverance
Or 'deliverance for a short space,' or 'in a short time:' see Ezra 9:8.
12:15a words
Or 'acts;' Heb. dabar, as 1Chron. 29:29.

Or 'to no-gods:' see Gal. 4:8.

Or 'statues:' see 1Kings 14:23.

Cushite.

Elsewhere, 'camp.'

Heb. 'many days.'

Lit. 'house of the stocks.'

Portion of Jerusalem lying south-west of the temple mount.

Heb. cors. * A cor is a liquid and a dry measure of equal capacity to the homer: it equals 10 ephahs or baths -- see 1Kings 4:22; Ezek. 45:11, 14; and Luke 16:7.

Or 'disposed.'
