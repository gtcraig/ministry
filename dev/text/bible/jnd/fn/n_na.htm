THE HOLY BIBLE Darby's Translation
Darby's Notes on Nahum
1:1h *
(The title of this book, 'Nahum'), Said to mean 'Comfort' or 'Comforted.'
1:7a trouble
Or 'distress,' as Gen. 35:3.
1:8b, 1:9b end
See Zeph. 1:18.
1:8c thereof
i.e. of Nineveh.
1:11d counsellor
Lit. 'a counsellor of Belial.'
1:12e thee
i.e. Judah: see ver. 15.
1:14f, 2:5f thee
i.e. the Assyrian, 'the wicked one,' ch. 1:15.
1:15g *
In the Hebrew, ch. 2 begins here.
1:15h one
Lit. 'Belial.'
2:1i pieces
Or 'The maul.'
2:2k again
Or 'hath returned to.'
2:2l Israel
Or 'hath turned away the pride of Jacob as the pride of Israel.'
2:2m them
Or 'the emptiers have emptied them out.'
2:3n his
i.e. of Jehovah, who sends them to besiege Nineveh.
2:3o sheen
Lit. 'fire.'
2:3p spears
Lit. 'cypresses.'
2:5q shelter
Shelter for the besieged.
2:7r she
i.e. Nineveh.
2:7s breasts
Lit. 'hearts.'
3:8a No-Amon
i.e. the god Amon of No (Thebes) in Egypt.
3:8b rivers
Or 'canals.'
3:9c Ethiopia
'Cush.'
3:9d infinite
Or 'endless.'
3:9e her
Lit. 'thy,' but referring here to No-Amon, which is apostrophised.
3:11f hid
Or 'shalt hide thyself.'
3:15g cankerworm
See Joel 1:4.
3:17h grasshoppers
Heb. gob gobai, lit. 'grasshopper of grasshoppers,' -- probably a species of locust which issues from the ground when hatched; not as Ex. 10:13.
