THE HOLY BIBLE Darby's Translation
Darby's Notes on Philemon
1:1e fellow-workman
Or 'our beloved and fellow-workman.'
1:7a through
Epi: 'by occasion of:' see Rom. 10:19. It was the condition of his joy.
1:9b Christ
Or 'Christ Jesus,' according to some authorities.
1:13c for
Huper: 'instead of thee.'
1:13d minister
diakoneo.
1:17e partner
Koinonos. as Heb. 2:14.
1:22f lodging
Or 'prepare hospitality for me;' that is, 'to receive me as a guest.'
