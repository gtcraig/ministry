THE HOLY BIBLE Darby's Translation
Darby's Notes on Nehemiah
1:1c Nehemiah
Meaning, 'Comfort of Jehovah.'
1:1d year
That is, of Artaxerxes Longimanus; see ch. 2:1; Ezra 7:1.
1:1e Shushan
The capital of Elam.
1:1f fortress
Or 'palace.'
1:6a time
Or 'this day.'
2:1b Artaxerxes
See Ezra 7:1.
2:3c place
Lit. 'house.'
2:8d forest
Heb. 'paradise,' 'park' or 'pleasure-grounds.'
2:8e palace
Or 'fortress.'
2:10a Horonite
According to some, 'of Beth-Horon,' Josh. 16:3, 5; according to others, 'of Horonaim in Moab,' Isa. 15:5
2:13b valley-gate
Gate of the valley or ravine of Hinnom.
2:15c valley
Or 'torrent:' see Deut. 2:13.
2:16d rulers
See Ezra 9:2.
2:19e Geshem
Gashmu: see ch. 6:6.
3:2f them
Lit. 'him.'
3:3g Senaah
Or 'Hassenaah.'
3:4h Koz
Or 'Hakkoz,' also ver. 21.
3:7a seat
Or 'throne;' as Gen. 41:40.
3:7b governor
Or 'pacha;' see Ezra 8:36.
3:8c, 3:12c, 3:23c them
Lit. 'him.'
3:8d perfumers
Lit. 'son of perfumers.'
3:8e as
Others, 'the Chaldeans had left the walls of Jerusalem as far as.'
3:15f Shelah
Siloam, John 9:7.
3:15g city
The part of Jerusalem thus called.
3:19h angle
Or 'turning:' so vers. 20, 24, 25.
3:20i, 3:21i, 3:24i another
Heb. 'a second.'
3:22k Jordan
See Gen. 13:10.
3:26a Nethinim
See Note, 1Chron. 9:2.
3:26b Ophel
See 2Chron. 27:3
3:29c, 3:30c them
Lit. 'him.'
3:30d chamber
Lit. 'lodging.'
3:31e the
Lit. 'son of the.'
3:31f place
Lit. 'house.'
3:31g, 3:32g of
Or 'upper chamber in.'
4:4h prey
Or 'to reproach.'
4:6i mind
Lit. 'heart.'
4:8k hinder
Or 'harm.'
4:11l adversaries
Tzar, as Ezra 4:1.
4:12m returned
Heb. 'ye return.'
4:14a, 4:19a, 5:7a rulers
As Ezra 9:2.
4:16b shields
Small shields.
4:22c servant
Heb. 'young man;' and so ver. 23 and ch. 5:15, 16.
4:23d side
Others. '... in going to the water;' probably a corrupt reading.
5:2e procure
Or 'let us procure.'
5:5f hand
As Gen. 31:29.
5:8a redeemed
Strictly, 'bought.'
5:11b day
Or 'at once.'
5:14c bread
* i.e. what he was entitled to as governor (pacha) of Jerusalem.
5:17d rulers
See Ezra 9:2.
6:2e plain
Or 'valley:' see Josh. 11:8.
6:5a servant
Heb. 'young man.'
6:10b night
Or 'to-night.'
6:19c deeds
Lit. 'kindnesses;' cf. ch. 9:25. Not chasidim, which would be 'pious deeds,' as ch. 13:14.
6:19d words
Or 'matters.'
7:5a rulers
See Ezra 9:2.
7:5b register
See Ezra 2.
7:6c children
Lit. 'sons,' and so throughout the chapter.
7:26d men
Enosh; and so to ver. 33.
7:52a Meunim
Or 'the Maonites:' see Ezra 2:50.
7:52b Nephishesim
Or 'the Nephusites:' see Ezra 2:50.
7:63c Hobaiah
Habaiah, Ezra 2:61.
7:65d Tirshatha
See Note a, Ezra 2:63.
7:65e Thummim
See Ex. 28:30; Lev. 8:8; Num. 27:21; Deut. 33:8; 1Sam. 28:6; Ezra 2:63.
7:71a pounds
Heb. maneh, as Ezra 2:69.
7:73b cities
Ch. 8 begins here in A.V. See Ezra 3:1.
8:18a read
Or 'one read;' 'there was reading.'
9:5b eternity
See 1Chron. 16:36; Ps. 41:13; 106:48; Dan. 2:20.
9:6c, 9:7c Same
Or 'Thou art He,' an expression which is really a name of God: see Deut. 32:39.
9:13a judgments
Or 'ordinances.'
9:15b sworn
Lit. 'lifted up thy hand.'
9:16c our
Or 'they and our.'
9:17d among
Lit. 'with.'
9:17e forgive
Lit. 'a God of pardons.'
9:17f loving-kindness
Chesed -- mercy: so ch. 13:22. See 2Chron. 6:42.
9:25a good, goodness
See Note c, ch. 6:19.
9:34b them
Or 'in the midst of them.'
9:36c servants
Or 'bondmen.'
10:31a debt
Lit. 'the debt of every hand,' i.e. for which any had given his hand. Ex. 23:10, 11; Deut. 15:1, 2.
10:32b ordinances
Lit. Lit. 'commandments.'
10:33c holy
Kodesh: see Note, Ex. 25:8. It might read 'consecrated things.'
10:37a meal
Or 'dough.' as Num. 15:20; Ezek. 44:30.
11:5b Shiloni
Or 'of the Shilonites:' see 1Chron. 9:5.
11:9c Senuah
Or 'Hassenuah.'
11:10d of
See 1Chron. 9:10.
11:11e ruler
Or 'overseer.'
11:14a Gedolim
Or 'Haggedolim.'
11:24b hand
i.e. at Shushan.
11:25c hamlets
Or 'enclosures,' 'farms;' Gen. 25:16.
11:25d villages
Lit. 'daughters,' also in vers. 27, 28, 30, 31, and elsewhere often.
12:9a watches
Or 'in their charges.'
12:12b of
Or 'for;' and so all through this passage.
12:17c Moadiah
Maadiah, ver. 5
12:22d, 12:23d Johanan
Jonathan, ver. 11.
12:22e until
Or 'under.'
12:22f Persian
Darius III. Codomanus; according to those who translate 'under the reign,' it would be Darius II, Nothus.
12:29g Beth-Gilgal
Or 'the house of Gilgal.'
12:29h hamlets
See ch. 11:25.
12:40a rulers
See Ezra 9:2.
12:43b offered
Zevach: lit. 'sacrificed.'
12:44c time
Or 'on that day.'
12:47d consecrated
Or 'set apart as holy.'
13:1e day
Or 'at that time,' as ch. 12:44.
13:4f chambers
Lit. 'chamber.'
13:11a rulers
See Ezra 9:2.
13:11b together
i.e. the Levites and singers.
13:12c storehouses
Elsewhere 'treasuries.'
13:14d deeds
Chasidim, as 2Chron. 32:32; 35:26: see 2Sam. 22:26.
13:19e servants
Lit. 'young men,' as elsewhere.
13:30a service
Lit. 'work.'
