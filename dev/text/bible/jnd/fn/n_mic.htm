THE HOLY BIBLE Darby's Translation
Darby's Notes on Micah
1:1b *
(The title of this Book, 'Micah'), Meaning, Who is like Jah? see ch. 7:18; Ps. 68:4.
1:8a stripped
Or 'barefooted.'
1:10b all
Or 'in Acco (i.e. 'weeping') weep not.' Vers. 10-15 are made up of paranomasia, see Isa. 5:7
1:10c Beth-le-aphrah
House of dust.
1:11d Shaphir
Beautiful town.
1:11e Zaanan
Coming forth, Josh. 15:37.
1:11f Beth-ezel
Neighbouring house.
1:11g will
Or '... forth; the lamentation of Beth-ezel will.'
1:11h shelter
Or 'stay:' lit. 'standing ground.'
1:12i Maroth
Bitterness.
1:14k Moresheth-Gath
Possession of Gath.
1:14l Achzib
Lying.
1:15m heir
Or, 'the possessor.'
1:15n Mareshah
Possession, inheritance.
1:15o Adullam
Or 'Even unto Adullam (meaning 'retreat') shall he come, -- the glory of Israel.'
1:16p eagle
Or 'vulture.'
2:1q work
See Note m, Ps. 5:5.
2:1r hand
Or 'because for them their hand is God (El):' see Prov. 3:27.
2:2s oppress
Or 'defraud,' see Ezek. 22:12.
2:2t man
Geber, Job 3:3.
2:2u man
Ish, Gen. 2:23.
2:6v Prophesy
Lit. 'Drop,' in this and ver. 11, as Amos 7:16.
2:6w these
i.e. If the true prophets do not prophesy to the unjust rich men (see vers. 1, 2): others translate 'They shall not prophesy of such.'
2:7x impatient
Or 'Is the Spirit of Jehovah straitened?'
2:7y to
Lit. 'with.'
2:8a with
Lit. 'from off.'
2:8b averse
Or 'as coming back.'
2:10c that
Or 'because it is defiled, it.'
2:12d Bozrah
Or 'in a fold.'
3:5e prepare
Lit. 'hallow,' as Jer. 6:4; 51:27, 28; Joel 3:9.
3:9f judgment
Or 'justice.'
3:12g forest
See Jer. 26:18.
4:1h But
Or 'And:' see Isa. 2:2-4.
4:3a many
Or 'great.'
4:6b halteth
Or 'limpeth,' as Gen. 32:31; Zeph. 3:19.
4:8c flock
Or 'Migdal-eder,' Gen. 35:21.
4:13d brass
Or 'bronze.'
5:2e *
In the Hebrew, ch. 5 begins here.
5:2f be
Or 'though thou be small.'
5:4a earth
Or 'land.'
5:5b princes
As Ps. 83:11; Ezek. 32:30; &c.: cf. also 'chiefs,' Josh. 13:21.
5:6c waste
Or 'feed on,' or 'rule,' Ps. 49:14; 80:13.
5:9d upon
Or 'above.'
5:9e adversaries
Or 'oppressors.'
5:15f of
Or 'who have not hearkened.'
6:4g bondage
Lit. 'bondmen.'
6:5h righteousness
Lit. 'righteousnesses.'
6:8a goodness
Chesed, 'mercy,' 'loving-kindness,' as 2Chron. 6:42.
6:10b measure
Lit. 'ephah.'
6:14c thee
Or 'thou shalt be quite cast down.'
6:16d desolation
Or 'an astonishment.'
6:16e thereof
i.e. Samaria, ch. 1:5.
7:1f desired
Or 'my soul desired an early fig.'
7:2g godly
Chasid: see Note, 2Chron. 6:42.
7:2h land
Or 'earth.'
7:2i men
The Adam -- mankind, as Gen. 1:27.
7:2k net
Or 'as after one under a ban:' see Isa. 34:5.
7:3l combine
Lit. 'weave,' 'twist.'
7:8m enemy
Feminine: see ver. 10.
7:8n arise
* Lit. 'when I have fallen I have arisen.'
7:11a limit
Or 'bound.'
7:12b Egypt
Matsor: Isa. 19:6.
7:12c river
i.e. the Euphrates.
7:14d forest
See Ezek. 34:25.
7:17e close
Or 'fortified.'
7:20f perform
Lit. 'give.'
7:20g Abraham
Or 'wilt give the truth promised to Jacob, the loving-kindness promised to Abraham.'
