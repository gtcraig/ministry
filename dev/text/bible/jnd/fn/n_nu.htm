THE HOLY BIBLE Darby's Translation
Darby's Notes on Numbers
1:16a summoned
That is, those who were habitually called to undertake the matters of the assembly.
1:20a generations
As Gen. 2:4, throughout this chapter.
3:1a generations
As Gen. 2:4.
3:3b consecrated
See Ex. 28:41.
3:9c of
Or 'on behalf of.'
3:16d commandment
Lit. 'mouth:' as very frequently in Numbers.
3:25a curtain
Or 'covering,' so ver. 26, and ch. 4:25, 26; see also Ex. 26:36.
3:31b curtain
Masach, as in ver. 25, but here the 'veil' as in Ex. 26:31.
4:3a service
Tsaba. Service to which one is subjected: elsewhere 'labour,' 'suffering,' 'military service,' and so 'labour' in vers. 30, 35, 39, 43: see ch. 26:2; Isa. 40:2.
4:5b separation
Masach; see Ex. 35:12.
4:9a light
As Ex. 25:6.
4:12b sanctuary
Kodesh; see Ex. 25:8.
4:13c ashes
See Lev. 1:16.
4:15d things
Or 'the sanctuary:' so in ver. 20. Same as 'sanctuary' vers. 12, 15, 16.
4:16e incense
See Ex. 30:7.
4:19f things
Or 'unto the holy of holies;' see Ex. 26:33.
4:20g moment
Lit. 'like a swallowing (of spittle):' Job 7:19.
4:23h service
Or 'work,' as ch. 3:7, 8.
4:27a appoint
As 'number,' ver. 32.
4:30b, 4:35b, 4:39b, 4:43b labour
See Note at ver. 3.
4:32c materials
As 'instruments' above.
4:37d through
Lit. 'by the hand of,' here and often elsewhere.
5:8a kinsman
'One who has the right of redemption,' as Lev. 25:25.
5:15b offering
Corban. Lev. 1:2.
5:19a husband
Or 'if thou being under thy husband hast not gone astray in uncleanness.'
5:26b burn
See Lev. 1:9, Note i.
6:2c Nazarite
'Nazarite,' 'consecration,' 'separation' in this chapter are from the same Hebrew root nazar, see Lev. 21:12. Used also for 'crown,' 2Kings 11:12; Ps. 89:39; 132:18, and for 'diadem,' Ex. 29:6; Lev. 8:9.
6:4a seed-stones
Others, 'sour grapes.'
6:5b grow
Grow freely.
6:12c forfeited
Lit. 'fall.'
6:26a give
Lit. 'set,' 'put on.'
7:3b offering
Corban. Lev. 1:2.
7:15c lamb
A male lamb; and so throughout the chapter.
8:2a lightest
See Ex. 25:37.
8:7b sin
See ch. 19:9.
8:11c offer
Lit. 'wave:' so vers. 13, 15, 21, and Lev. 7:30.
8:24a labour
See ch. 4:23.
9:7a offering
Corban, Lev. 1:2, 'a thing presented.'
9:14b stranger
Or 'sojourner,' as Ex. 12:48; Lev. 20:2.
9:17c still
Lit. 'dwelt,' 'abode:' so ver. 18; ch. 10:12; see Ex. 24:16.
9:21d cloud
Others read 'whether it was by day or by night when the cloud.'
9:22e days
Elsewhere also 'a year.'
10:2a together
See Ex. 12:16.
10:9b enemy
Tzar. Or 'oppressor.'
10:10c moons
Or 'the beginnings of your months.'
10:12d journeys
Or 'their marching order.'
10:30a kindred
Or 'birthplace.'
11:1b evil
Or 'that the people became like men complaining of evil.'
11:3c Taberah
'Burning.'
11:7d appearance
Lit. 'eye.'
11:8a oil-cakes
Others, 'fresh oil.'
11:25b it
So the LXX. The A.V. 'and ceased not,' follows a different punctuation of the Hebrew.
11:28a men
Or 'select ones;' others, 'an attendant from his youth up.'
11:31b sea
Or the west.
11:34c Kibroth-hattaavah
Graves of lust.
12:1d Ethiopian
Or 'Cushite.'
12:2e to
Or 'through.'
12:3f meek
Or 'lowly,' 'humble.'
12:8g openly
Or 'visibly.'
12:14a shut
Or 'shut up.'

Lit. 'men, heads.'

Deliverance.

Jehovah is Saviour.

Or 'by the south country:' see Gen. 12:9.
13:21f towards
Or 'at the entrance of.'
13:24g Eshcol
Valley of the bunch of grapes.

Lit. 'lifted up and gave their voice.'
14:5b congregation
See Note, Ex. 12:3.
14:9c defence
Lit. 'shade.'
14:11d believe
Or 'trust.'
14:12a destroy
Elsewhere 'dispossess.'
14:23b, 14:30b wise
Lit. 'if they shall: ' a strong testifying negative in Hebrew, already noticed, Gen. 21:23; and see Num. 32:11; Heb. 3:11.
14:24c in
Lit. 'with.'
14:28d live
See Note, Gen. 24:38; 'saith' is as Gen. 22:16.

'feed,' as 1Sam. 25:16.

Lit. 'from after.'

Lit. 'rose up presumptuously.'

or 'calf.'

Or 'fringes' or 'a flower,' i.e. to attract attention.

Or 'sinned against.'

See Ex. 29:13.
16:40c him
That is, Eleazar.
