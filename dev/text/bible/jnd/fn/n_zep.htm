THE HOLY BIBLE Darby's Translation
Darby's Notes on Zephaniah
1:1d *
(The title of this book, 'Zephaniah'), Said to mean 'Whom Jehovah hides' or 'treasures.'
1:4e Chemarim
See 2Kings 23:5.
1:5f Malcham
Some regard this as meaning Milcom; others that, as being Hebrew for 'their king,' it means Baal. Cf. Jer. 49:1.
1:7g hallowed
See Isa. 13:3.
1:9a house
Or 'masters' houses.'
1:10b quarter
See 2Kings 22:14.
1:11c Maktesh
Meaning, 'Mortar:' name of the lower, basin-like, part of Jerusalem.
1:11d Canaan
Or 'merchant people:' see Zech. 14:21.
1:17e men
Adam -- mankind.
1:18f end
Or 'consumption:' see Isa. 10:22, 23; Jer. 4:27, &c.; Ezek. 11:13; Nah. 1:8.
2:1g shame
Or 'longing.'
2:5h Cherethites
Ezek. 25:16.
2:6i cave-dwellings
Or, 'shall be meadows, shepherds' cisterns.'
2:7k captivity
See Note, Ps. 126:1.
2:11a famish
Or, 'will annihilate.'
2:12b Ethiopians
'Cushites.'
2:14c flocks
Or 'herds.'
2:14d beasts
Or 'numbers of beasts of every kind.'
2:14e bare
Or 'he hath broken off.'
2:15f security
Or 'carelessly.'
3:5g Jehovah
Or 'Jehovah is righteous.'
3:5h it
Or 'he.'
3:7i fear
Or 'Surely thou wilt fear.'
3:8k determination
Or 'judgment.'
3:9l language
Lit. 'lip.'
3:9m consent
Lit. 'with one shoulder.'
3:11a of
Or 'on.'
3:11b mountain
See Ezek. 7:20, Note.
3:18c assemblies
Or 'set feast.'
3:19d halted
See Mic. 4:6
3:19e shame
Lit. 'in every land of their shame.'
