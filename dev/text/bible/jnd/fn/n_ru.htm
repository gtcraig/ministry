THE HOLY BIBLE Darby's Translation
Darby's Notes on Ruth
1:1a ruled
Shaphat, lit. 'judged.'
1:1b country
Lit. 'fields,' 'farmlands,' as ver. 6, and elsewhere. As 2Kings 8:3, 5.
1:2c Elimelech
Meaning, 'whose God is king.'
1:2d Naomi
Meaning, 'my pleasantness.'
1:20a Mara
Meaning, 'bitter,' or 'bitterness.'
2:1b relation
Or 'friend,' 'acquaintance.'
2:1c Boaz
Meaning, 'in him is strength.'
2:3d allotment
Lit. 'portion of the field.'
2:5e servant
Or 'young man:' also ver. 6, as Judg. 19:3 and 9.
2:10f foreigner
Or 'stranger.'
2:13g to
Lit. 'spoken to the heart of.'
2:13h handmaid
Or 'bondwoman.'
2:16a out
According to others, 'let fall.'
2:16b handfuls
Or 'sheaves;' the word is only found in this place.
2:20c redemption
See Lev. 25:25.
3:3d Wash
Or 'bathe,' as elsewhere.
3:9e skirt
Lit. 'wing,' as Ex. 19:4; Ps. 36:7.
3:15a went
Some read 'she went.'
4:4b thought
Lit. 'said.'
4:4c thee
Lit. 'uncover thine ear:' so in 1Sam. 9:15; 20:2, 12, 13.
4:4d inhabitants
Or 'before those that sit here.'
4:5e field
As 'country,' ch. 1:1.
4:14a redemption
Or 'without a redeemer;' it refers to the son born, i.e. Obed.
4:17b Obed
Meaning, 'worshipper' or 'servant.'
4:18c generations
As Gen. 2:4
4:20d Salmon
Or Salma.
