THE HOLY BIBLE Darby's Translation
Darby's Notes on Amos
1:1h *
(The title of this Book, 'Amos'), Said to mean, 'Burden-bearer.'
1:1i herdmen
Or 'sheepmasters,' 2Kings 3:4.
1:1k Tekoa
In Judah, 2Chron. 11:6.
1:2a pastures
or 'habitations:' see Jer. 9:10; 23:10; 25:37, &c.
1:5b bar
See Deut. 3:5.
1:5c valley
Or 'plain.' see Note, Josh. 11:8.
1:5d Aven
Or 'valley of idols.'
1:5e Beth-Eden
Meaning, 'House of delight.'
1:6f sentence
Lit. 'revoke it:' see ver. 3.
1:8g Jehovah
Adonai Jehovah: see Note b, Ezek. 2:4.
1:11h pity
Lit. 'destroyed his mercies.'
2:7a after
Or, 'they who pant after.'
2:8b condemned
Or 'those that are amerced.'
2:11c saith
See Isa. 1:24.
2:13d you
Lit. 'in your place.' Others, 'I am pressed under you (i.e. 'your sins') as the cart is loaded with sheaves.'
2:14e flight
Or 'refuge,' as Jer. 25:35.
3:6f it
Or 'not have acted.'
3:9g in
Or 'upon.'
3:12a couch
The best place of the divan in the houses of the rich.
3:13b in
Or 'against.'
4:3c Harmon
Some translate 'to the mountains,' others, 'to the enemy's fortress,' or 'to the palace,' as in ch. 1:4, &c.: the meaning is not ascertained.
4:4d days
Perhaps 'years;' 'days' in Hebrew is used for 'years,' but here probably it is in irony.
4:5e burn
'Burn as incense:' see Lev. 1:9.
4:9f palmer-worm
See Joel 1:4.
5:3a forth
i.e. that contributed a thousand men for war: cf. Judg. 11:3 and Note.
5:5b nought
The Hebrew for 'nought' is here Aven, 'vanity,' 'iniquity:' see Hos. 4:15.
5:11c pleasant
Or 'excellent.'
5:12d bribe
1Sam. 12:3.
5:22a offer
Lev. 14:20.
5:26b Moloch
i.e. Milcom. Others render it 'your King.'
5:26c Chiun
Some understand Chiun as the 'stand of the idol.' The LXX reads, 'star of your god Remphan:' see Acts 7:43.
6:1d secure
Or 'careless,' as Isa. 32:9.
6:1e first
Or 'named the chief;' but cf. Num. 24:20.
6:6f breach
Or 'destruction:' see Lam. 3:48.
6:7g captive
Lit. 'at the head of the captives'
6:8h himself
Lit. 'his soul.'
6:10i uncle
Or 'next relation.'
6:13k power
Lit. 'horns.'
6:14a Arabah
Deut. 1:1 and 2Kings 14:25.
7:1b locusts
Heb. gob: see Nah. 3:17.
7:4c inheritance
Lit. 'lot,' 'portion,' Num 18:20.
7:14d gatherer
Or 'dresser.'
7:16e words
Lit. 'drop not:' see Job 29:22; Ezek. 20:46; 21:2; Mic. 2:6, 11.
8:3f palace
Or 'temple.'
8:3a Silence
See ch. 6:10.
8:4b poor
Lit. 'meek,' or 'afflicted,' as Ps. 9:18.
8:9c land
Or 'the earth.'
8:14d sin
Elsewhere also 'trespass.'
9:1e them
Or 'their posterity,' as ch. 4:2; Ps. 109:13.
9:7f Ethiopians
Heb. 'Cushites.'
9:7a Caphtor
See Gen. 10:14.
9:11b tabernacle
Or 'booth.' 'hut,' 2Sam. 11:11; Ps. 76:2.
9:12c Edom
The LXX and their followers would seem to have read 'men,' and hence the application in Acts 15:17. But the manuscripts with one voice have 'Edom.' There can be no doubt that it is right.
9:14d turn
Ps. 14:7 and 126:1.
