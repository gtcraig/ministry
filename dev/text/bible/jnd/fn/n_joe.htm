THE HOLY BIBLE Darby's Translation
Darby's Notes on Joel
1:1g *
(The title of this book, 'Joel'), Said to mean, 'Jehovah is God.'
1:4a palmer-worm, cankerworm
Probably different species of locust, or in different stages of growth, as 'grub,' 'creeper,' 'feeder.' See Note, Ps. 105:34.
1:4b caterpillar
Or 'devourer:' see Ps. 78:46; some think the 'locust' in its winged state.
1:10c, 1:12c up
Or 'is ashamed.'
1:14d elders
Or 'old men,' as ver. 2.
1:15e Almighty
See Note, Isa. 13:6.
2:1f trumpet
Or 'horn.'
2:8a amid
Or 'burst through.'
2:8b wounded
Or 'separated,' or 'they break not off their course.'
2:12c saith
See Gen. 22:16.
2:13d loving-kindness
See Ex. 34:6.
2:14e repent
Or 'repent again.'
2:15f trumpet
Or 'horn.'
2:16g children
Or 'infants.'
2:17h nations
Or 'that the nations should rule over them.'
2:20i hinder
Or 'western:' see Note c, Deut. 11:24.
2:22a increase
Lit. 'strength.'
2:23b measure
Or 'richly.' Lit. 'in righteousness.'
2:23c rain
See Note f, Ps. 84:6.
2:23d season
Or 'in the first month.'
2:28e *
Vers. 28-32 compose ch. 3 in the Hebrew.
2:30f shew
Lit. 'give.'
2:32g deliverance
Or 'an escape,' as Jer. 25:35.
3:2h Jehoshaphat
Meaning, 'Jehovah judgeth.'
3:6i Greeks
Heb. Javanim, Gen. 10:2.
3:9a prepare
Lit. 'hallow,' 'sanctify,' as Jer. 6:4.
3:11b ye
Or 'Band together.'
3:11c ones
Gibbor: see Judg. 5:13; Job 3:3.
3:14d decision
Or 'what is determined,' as Isa. 10:22, &c.
3:16e refuge
Or 'stronghold,' 'fortress:' see Jer. 16:19.
3:18f water-courses
Or 'ravines.'
3:18g Shittim
Valley of acacias.
